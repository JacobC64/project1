Welcome to Basement JiuJitsu!

The premier location for your Brazilian JiuJitsu and Mixed Martial Arts training in Northern Virginia.

We offer high level classes in Brazilian JiuJitsu, Muay Thai, Boxing, Wrestling, Tai Chi, Wing Chung, Yoga, and more! All taught under the world famous 8th degree Black Belt Mark P. Chadley. His belt lineage can be traced back to Mitsuyo Maeda, the creator of JiuJitsu, himself! Do not let that level of experience intimidate you though because BasementJJ is meant for all levels, no matter althletic ability, age, or gender! All are accepted!

Martial Arts is a great way to get exercise, be it to lose weight or gain muscle, there is no better way to have fun and exercise at the same time. You will be able to push your body at your own pace, while your mind gains valuble lessions in discipline and self-defense. It is a great way to learn how to defend yourself in life threatening situations and gain the patience to know when you are in such a situation.

We also offer training for those who wish to train for MMA. With our techniques you can build an arsenal to take into your MMA career. We can also offer strength and conditioning coaching, fight preperation, and cornering for fights. So if you wish to pursue the championship you can start here!

Whatever your reason for wanting to train it can be fulfilled at Basement JiuJitsu! Sign up now and you will recieve one free class to be redeemed whenever.
