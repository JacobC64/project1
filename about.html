Our Beginnings

We started out with humble beginnings, just a group of friends with no where to train once COVID-19 caused all of the gyms to close down. So we decided that we should make our own gym. At first it was as simple as all pitching in to buy some mats to cover the basement of our friend Mark P. Chadley's home. Under his guidance we began training and more of our friends wanted to join. We decieded that it would be a great way for our friends to spend time together, learn our passion for MMA, and get great exercise.

As more and more friends started to join, we thought that we should get more equipment. We bought better mats, practice dummies, BJJ Gis, punching bags, and more. Soon our little basement started to fill out and look like an actual MMA gym. Soon we could not hold all of the people that wanted in on the gym so we moved on the better basements and equipment and soon we let people outside our friend group join.

Our origins are from friends who just wanted to train together and have fun, but what it became was a place for people to learn about themselves and grow. We were excited to share our passion with our friends and we are even more excited to share our passion with you! So please come visit us and change your life, athletically, mentally, and spirtually. Anyone is welcome!
